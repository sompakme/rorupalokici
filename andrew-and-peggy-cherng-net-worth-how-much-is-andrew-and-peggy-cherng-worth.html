Early Life and Education

Peggy Tsiang Cherng, a prominent figure in the business world, was born in Myanmar and spent her formative years in Hong Kong. Her educational journey led her to the United States, where she pursued her academic goals. Peggy enrolled at Baker University in Kansas, where fate would have it that she would meet her future business partner and husband, Andrew Cherng.

Ambitious and driven, Peggy transferred to Oregon State University before ultimately obtaining her bachelor’s degree in applied mathematics and a Ph.D. in electrical engineering from the University of Missouri. In 1982, Andrew and Peggy Cherng embarked on their entrepreneurial journey by opening the first Panda Express in Los Angeles County.
